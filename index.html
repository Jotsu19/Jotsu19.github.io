Who I Am

About Me

My name is Jaeron Josh M. Layupan, a 3rd year BSIT student from Kibangay, Lantapan, Bukidnon. I am a quiet and reserved person, but when it comes to the things I am passionate about, I give it my all.

I chose BSIT because it has always been my dream course. Growing up, we had an internet cafe in our place, and ever since then I became fascinated with computers and everything about them. That early experience sparked something in me that never went away.

I enjoy coding — even when it gets difficult. I believe that when you truly enjoy what you do, the hardships stop feeling like a burden. Outside of school, I love playing basketball — I was a varsity player in high school, and the sport taught me teamwork and discipline that I carry into my work.
